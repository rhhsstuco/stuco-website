Events This Month

15 Dec

Jollyweek

❄️ A week of fun, school-wide winter-related activities! ❄️

15 Dec

Light Up The Hill

A community-wide holiday celebration featuring activities representing Hanukkah, Christmas, Yalda Night, Kwanzaa, Lunar New Year, and more. School clubs and music departments will host performances and hands-on activities for families and students. Staff and student families are welcome.

16 Dec

Farmer’s Market

A variety of school clubs will offer items for sale as part of the Jollyweek Market. Students must purchase Market Dollars to spend at the booths.

17 Dec

Gingerbread House Decorating Contest

Students are invited to participate in the Gingerbread House Decorating Challenge in groups of 3–5.

18 Dec

Holiday Bingo

A free, festive Winter Festival-themed Bingo event open to all students. Come play, have fun, and win prizes.

19 Dec

Hot Chocolate Day

Celebrate the final day before the holiday break with hot chocolate. Stop by the Student Council office to enjoy free warm drinks.
